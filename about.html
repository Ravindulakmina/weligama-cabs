Our story

Started by a driver. Run by drivers.

Weligama Cabs was started in 2014 by Suresh — a tuk-tuk driver who got tired of seeing tourists overpay and underwhelmed. He bought a second-hand Toyota Axio, taught two friends English, and began doing airport runs on fixed fares.

Twelve years later, we're a small fleet of nine vehicles and eleven drivers — every one of them either from Weligama, Mirissa, or Ahangama. We've stayed deliberately small because we believe doing fewer trips better is more important than scaling for the sake of it.

If you book a ride with us, the person who replies to your WhatsApp is the same person who personally briefed your driver on the route, your name, and your flight. There's no call centre, no faceless app, no booking fee.

What we stand for

Four things we won't compromise.

On time, every time

We'd rather be 15 minutes early than 60 seconds late. Especially for airport pickups.

Honest answers
