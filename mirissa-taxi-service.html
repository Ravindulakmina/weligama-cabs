FAQ

Mirissa taxi questions, answered.

How much is a taxi from Mirissa to CMB airport?

Fixed at $70 USD in a sedan (up to 3 pax, 2 bags), $85 in an SUV, or $100 in an 8-seat van. The fare is door-to-door, includes the expressway toll, and is locked in at booking.

Can you do a 4 a.m. whale-watching pickup?

Absolutely — it's one of our most-booked rides. $8 from anywhere in Mirissa to the harbour. If you're staying in Weligama, we'll come down for $10.

How early should I book a Mirissa taxi in high season?

For airport runs in December–February, ideally 2–3 days ahead. For local hops (Coconut Tree Hill, Galle, etc.) the morning of is fine — message us by 9 p.m. the night before for early starts.

Do you wait while we go on the whale-watching boat?

We don't — the harbour parking is terrible at peak hours. Instead, message your driver when you're heading back to shore, and they'll come down to collect you. Pickup-from-harbour costs the same $8.
