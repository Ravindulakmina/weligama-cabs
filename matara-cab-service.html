Matara taxi service

Matara, on the meter we don't use.

Most travellers skip Matara, and we think that's a mistake. The Old Dutch fort, the cliff-top lighthouse, Polhena reef's shallow snorkelling, and Star Fort are all here — and so is a proper local food scene that hasn't been sanitised for visitors. We can pick you up from anywhere on the south coast, and we know the parking situation in town better than the locals.

Things to do

What we'll happily drive you to in Matara.

If you're hiring us for the half-day or full day, here's where most of our guests ask to be taken.

Matara Lighthouse

The southern-most lighthouse in Sri Lanka. Best at sunset when the cliffs glow.

SunsetFree entry

Polhena Reef Snorkelling

A shallow reef just off Polhena beach — turtles, parrotfish, soft coral. Great for first-time snorkellers.

SnorkellingBeginner-friendly

Star Fort

A tiny but perfectly-preserved Dutch fort in the centre of town. 20-min visit, often empty.

HeritageQuick stop

Parey Dewa Temple

A Buddhist shrine on a tiny island connected to the beach by a footbridge. Beautiful at sunrise.

FAQ

Matara taxi questions, answered.

How long is Matara to Colombo airport by taxi?

Around 3 hours 15 minutes via the E01 Southern Expressway, plus a 20-min run from your address to the Matara expressway entrance. For 6 a.m. flights we recommend leaving by 1 a.m.

Can you do a half-day Matara sightseeing tour?

Yes — a typical half-day covers the lighthouse, Star Fort, Polhena, and a stop at the market. Fixed at $40 for the half-day (4 hr), $70 for the full day.

Is Polhena good for kids to snorkel?

It's one of the best beginner snorkelling spots on the south coast — knee-deep at low tide with a clear reef edge. We can recommend a snorkel rental place that doesn't overcharge.

Where do you wait while we visit?

Star Fort and the lighthouse both have nearby parking. For Polhena, the driver waits at a café across the road — they're used to us.
