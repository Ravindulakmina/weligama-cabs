Frequently asked questions

How much is a taxi from Colombo Airport to Weligama?
A private taxi from Colombo Airport (CMB / Bandaranaike International Airport) to Weligama is a fixed price starting from USD 65 for a comfortable sedan. Vans are available for groups and surfboards from USD 85, mini buses from USD 120. The exact rate depends on vehicle type and group size — message us on WhatsApp for an instant quote.

How long does the journey take?
The drive from Colombo Airport to Weligama is around 2.5 to 3 hours, taking the Southern Expressway (E01) almost all the way. We make a short stop at a clean rest area roughly halfway if you'd like coffee or a bathroom break.

Can I book via WhatsApp?
Absolutely — WhatsApp is our most popular booking channel. Send us your pickup time, flight number and destination and you'll receive a confirmation with your driver's name and vehicle details, usually within minutes.

Do you offer airport pickup and meet & greet?
Yes. Your English-speaking driver will be waiting in the arrivals hall with a name board. Free flight tracking is included, so even delayed flights are covered with no extra charge.

Are prices fixed?
Yes — all our rates are fixed and confirmed in writing at the time of booking. No meters, no surge pricing, no last-minute add-ons. You'll know exactly what you'll pay before the trip starts.
