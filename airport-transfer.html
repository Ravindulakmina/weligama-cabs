Common questions

Frequently asked.

If your question isn't covered here, just message us — we usually reply in minutes, not hours.

What if my flight is delayed?

No charge. We track your flight from the moment it leaves your origin airport, and the driver will adjust accordingly. Up to 90 minutes of free wait time is included.

What if I land at 3 a.m.?

No surcharge for night arrivals. We operate 24/7, including all public holidays. The driver gets a slightly higher share of the fare for unsocial hours — that's already in the price you see.

Can I pay in foreign currency?

Yes — USD, EUR and GBP cash are all welcome at the day's market rate, alongside LKR, card or local bank transfer.

Do you do return transfers too?

Absolutely. If you book round-trip from the start we'll usually knock 10–15% off the second leg.

Are child seats included?

Yes, on request and at no extra charge. Just tell us the child's age when you book so we bring the right type.
