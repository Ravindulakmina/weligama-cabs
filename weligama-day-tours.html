Common questions

Frequently asked.

If your question isn't covered here, just message us — we usually reply in minutes, not hours.

Can I customise the itinerary?

Every tour on this page is a starting point. Tell us what you want to see (or avoid), how early you can wake up, and how active you want to be — we'll redesign the day around you.

How many people can join one tour?

Up to 3 in a sedan, 4 in an SUV, or 8 in a van. The fare is per vehicle, not per person, so larger groups get better value.

What's the best season for whale watching?

November to April is peak. May to October is monsoon and boats often don't go out. We'll be honest with you on the day-before forecast.

Do tours include entry tickets?

No — paying these directly keeps us out of the kickback game. We'll guide you through what's worth paying for (Yala safari, Sinharaja ranger) and what isn't (most gem shops).
