FAQ

Questions, Answered

How do I book a ride?

The fastest way is WhatsApp — send your pickup point, destination, date, time and number of passengers, and we'll confirm a fixed price within minutes. You can also use the quote button on this page.

Do you offer airport pickups?

Yes. We monitor your flight and your driver waits at arrivals with a name board — included free, even if your flight is delayed.

Can I pay by card?

We accept cash (LKR, USD, EUR, GBP), card payments and online bank transfers. For tours, a small deposit may be requested to secure your booking.

Are your prices fixed?

Always. The fare we quote includes fuel, tolls and the driver — no meters, no detours and no hidden extras. The only changes are ones you ask for, like an added stop.

Are child seats available?

Yes — just let us know your child's age when booking and we'll fit a suitable seat at no extra charge.
