FAQ

Questions, Answered

How do I book a ride?

The fastest way is WhatsApp — just send your pickup point, destination, date, time and number of passengers, and we'll confirm a fixed price within minutes. You can also use the quote form on this page.

Do you offer airport pickups?

Yes. We monitor your flight and your driver waits at arrivals with a name board — included free, even if your flight is delayed. Drop-offs to the airport are timed to get you there with comfortable margin.

Can I pay by card?

We accept cash (LKR, USD, EUR, GBP), card payments and online bank transfers. For tours and round trips a small deposit may be requested to secure your booking.

Are your prices fixed?

Always. The fare we quote includes fuel, tolls and the driver — there are no meters, no detours and no hidden extras. The only changes are ones you ask for, like an added stop.

Are child seats available?

Yes — just let us know your child's age when booking and we'll fit a suitable seat at no extra charge.
