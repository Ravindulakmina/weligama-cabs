Common questions

Frequently asked.

If your question isn't covered here, just message us — we usually reply in minutes, not hours.

How long is the drive from Colombo to Weligama?

Roughly 2 hr 45 min on the E01 expressway, traffic permitting. From central Colombo, allow an extra 20–30 min to reach the expressway entrance. From CMB airport, the run is 2 hr 30 min.

Can we make stops along the way?

Of course. Popular options are Galle Fort (~60 min), Hikkaduwa beach (~45 min), Madu River boat ride (~90 min), or a roadside lunch in Ambalangoda. Add stops at booking or just tell the driver on the day.

Is the expressway toll included?

Yes — every fare on this page already includes the toll. You'll never be asked to pay anything in cash unless you've added an optional extra.

Can you do this trip in a van for a group of six?

Yes — the Toyota KDH van comfortably seats up to 8 with luggage. It's $95 one-way or $170 round-trip.

What about return — Weligama back to Colombo?

Same fare structure in reverse. We strongly recommend booking the airport drop the night before — early-morning flights catch a lot of people out.
